DATA PORTRAIT: SCHEDULER

Description

Your calendar is sentient. It keeps you on track with periodic updates of what it is up to.

Going down your week, you:
Yellow - Class
Orange - Scheduling
Pink - Homework
Purple - Free
Blue - Rest

Note: to switch it up, click the mouse :)

Design Process

I do my scheduling with my phone, events to the Calendar and tasks to Notion. This sketch focuses on my Calendar notifications and tasks prompted by those alerts.
Note: classes have two alerts per class

Reflection

This drawing would improve if I could choose which points (the circles/tasks) to exclude per row (day).

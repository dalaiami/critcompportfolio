EXPERIMENTAL CLOCK: LIFELINE

Description

This was something we all need in our lives, a beacon of hope.

Is it time for boba yet?.

Design Process

Originally, I wanted a cone to fall over in increments (niche interest of mine), but then I changed my mind to making a boba countdown of sorts.

I used a PNG in my code to make it recognizable.

Reflection

I couldn't get this sketch to function how I wanted, even though the tutorials made sense in the moment--- ugh. The cup is supposed to 'fill' by a rectangle moving UP the canvas as seconds pass. But still, unlimited refills, so we're living the dream!

Credits

This project was created from my obsession with thai tea + boba. A quick fix from KT and Aarati saved the day.

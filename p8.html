EXPERIMENTAL CAMERA: VHS COME BACK TO ME

Description

We all miss the idea of VHS... right? Regardless of our current age, we were younger then, with probably less to worry about.
If not less, then different worries.
Let this be a break from anxieties.

Design Process

I had a hard time figuring out what I could do with p5 for this and if I could make my ideas happen. The Lab exercises helped, but once I was oon my own, I couldn't think of a simpler idea than I'd originally come up with.

The idea I ended up going with was of stripped down to the basics: the color, size of frame, text elements, and noise.
Note: Images coming soon

Reflection

I had trouble making the sketch load but, but overall I got the look I wanted for the VHS filter.

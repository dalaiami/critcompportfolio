FACE GENERATOR: THX LOL

Description

A cheerful face greets you. They're so excited that they can't keep form.

They say 'thanks for coming'.

Design Process

After deciding on a smiley face as my base element, I added text to give them context- to make their world.

Reflection

I made this grateful face, to have a friend embedded in the web.

Credits

This project was created from my obsession with thank you bags.

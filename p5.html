OPTICAL ILLUSION: WAHUM

Description

This piece has hidden text.

Can you find the illusion?

Design Process

I briefly looked at Islamic patterns and art to come up with my sketch. I wanted there to be text, but not religious text necessarily as a lot of examples had. I chose the word وهم (Wahum) which can translate to illusion, and hid it within the framing.

Reflection

This drawing went alright, with some hiccups in the coding becuase I still find it difficult to translate into JS. My idea was also hard to solidify.

AUTOBIO GAME: ANTISOCIAL AMI

Description

Do you also avoid phone calls as much as possible?
Face you fears* as Antisocial Ami, in this game.
+ An External Link to Play

Note: Click the sketch frame to turn on the background music, to calm you as you navigate social interactions.

Design Process

I grayboxed this game in color, since I kept losing focus when it was all actually gray. The jump from that version to the final, mostly involved adding instructions to the screen, adding images and sound, and finishing up with a font.

Reflection

Once I really got started, I could connect the tutorials more easily and use the code elements to get the game experience I planned. I had some difficulty with putting elements in olace but commenting them when not needed and slowing down helped me wokr around that. Also, indenting was really important for visualizing the code and collapsing the functions, etc.
